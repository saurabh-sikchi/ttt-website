Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

What's included in the trip price?

The trip price includes 9 days/8 nights accommodation (twin sharing basis), daily breakfast, all transfers mentioned in the itinerary, Tirta Empul Temple and Kecak Dance tickets, and daily Instagram spot photography assistance. It does not include flights to/from Bali, other meals not mentioned, personal expenses, or optional activities.

How many people will be on the trip?

Our trips typically have 12-16 travelers, creating an intimate group experience while still having enough people to make new friends. We carefully curate our groups to ensure a good mix of like-minded individuals.

Do I need a visa for Bali?

Indian passport holders can enter Indonesia (Bali) visa-free for up to 30 days. Your passport should be valid for at least 6 months beyond your planned departure date. We'll provide detailed visa information in your pre-departure materials.

What should I pack for this trip?

Pack light, breathable clothing for Bali's tropical climate. Essentials include swimwear, sunscreen, insect repellent, a sarong (for temple visits), comfortable walking shoes, and a light rain jacket. Don't forget your camera or smartphone for those Instagram-worthy shots! A detailed packing list will be provided before departure.

Is this trip suitable for solo travelers?

Absolutely! In fact, most of our travelers join solo. Our trips are designed to create a welcoming environment where it's easy to make friends. You'll be paired with another solo traveler of the same gender for accommodation (twin sharing), or you can opt for a single room for an additional fee.
