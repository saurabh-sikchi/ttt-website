Cancellation Policy

Important information about our cancellation terms and conditions

Initial Deposit

NO REFUND SHALL BE MADE WITH RESPECT TO THE INITIAL DEPOSIT AMOUNT FOR ANY CANCELLATIONS.

Cancellation Fees

If cancellations are made 60 days or more before the start date of the trip, 50% of the trip cost will be charged as cancellation fees.

If cancellations are made less than 60 days before the start date of the trip, 100% of the trip cost will be charged as cancellation fees.

Activity Cancellations

In the case of unforeseen weather conditions or government restrictions, certain activities may be canceled. In such cases, the operator will try their best to provide an alternate feasible activity. However, no refund will be provided for the same.

Important Notes

We strongly recommend purchasing travel insurance to protect your investment in case of unexpected cancellations.

All cancellation requests must be submitted in writing via email to info@tipsyturtletravels.com.

The date of cancellation is determined as the date on which we receive your written cancellation request.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our cancellation policy

Can I transfer my booking to someone else?

Yes, you may transfer your booking to another person if you are unable to travel. The new traveler must meet all the requirements for the trip and accept the terms and conditions. Please contact us at least 30 days before the trip start date to arrange a transfer.

What if I need to change my travel dates?

Date changes are treated as cancellations and rebookings. The standard cancellation policy will apply, and you will need to book a new trip at the current rates. We recommend contacting us as soon as possible if you need to change your dates.

What happens if Tipsy Turtle Travels cancels the trip?

In the rare event that we need to cancel a trip due to unforeseen circumstances (such as natural disasters, political instability, or insufficient participants), we will offer you the choice of a full refund or the option to transfer to another trip. We are not responsible for any incidental expenses that you may have incurred as a result of your booking, such as visas, vaccinations, or non-refundable flights.
